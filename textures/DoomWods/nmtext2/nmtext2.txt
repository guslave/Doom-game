Filename                : NMTEXT2.WAD

Author                  : Nick Baker  
		  
Email Address           : nbaker1@titus.force9.co.uk

Description		: 8 animated textures: Blood drips, a brown
			  slime fall, a forcefield, fire, a lava fall,
			  snow, water drips and a waterfall.

			  Note that there are no PNAMES or TEXTURE1
			  resources.  This is so that you can chose
			  between replacing existing animated textures,
			  or creating new ones via BOOM or DOSDoom.

Additional Credits to   : The author of Frost Bitten for the snow idea.
